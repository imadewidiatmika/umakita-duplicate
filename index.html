Our Home is a luxury 4-bedroom villa located just north of Ubud, close enough to enjoy the proximity and convenience of Bali’s cultural and spiritual centre, separate enough to feel the essence of the island’s timelessness among the sweeping padi terraces. Everything about this family friendly villa, from its contemporary style and broad interiors to its range of facilities, promises guests the ultimate in convenience and relaxation.

Our villa is ideally suited as a large family retreat or for groups of small families and friends, sleeping up to 8 adults comfortably and flexible for additional kids. Sink back in and relax for the duration, or use as a convenient hub to explore Ubud and the island. Choose to bring the island to you with the perks of pre-booked yoga instructors, massages or a bespoke chef. Umakita is serviced with full-time English-speaking staff to ensure your every need is met.
